FAQs & Customer Support

If you are facing any problems, please read the FAQs below. You will find answers & solutions to most of the common questions.
If your problem is still unresolved, please email us at support[at]E-vault[dot]gov[dot]in. Our support team will get back to you.
           
How can I sign up for E-vault?
Signing up for E-vault is easy - all you need is your mobile number.

Your mobile number will be authenticated by sending an OTP (one-time password) followed by selecting a username & password. This will create your 
E-vault account.

After your Evault account is successfully created, you can voluntarily provide your Aadhaar number (issued by UIDAI) to avail additional services 

What is OTP?
OTP is a random one-time password generated by the UIDAI system and sent to your registered mobile number and email-id. 
The OTP is valid for 30 mins only. If you enter the OTP after 30 mins, it will be rendered invalid and you will have to enter a fresh one.

I registered in E-vault but I want to change my username, is there any way to do so?
Username once created in E-vault cannot be changed in the current version of the application.

How can I upload documents to my E-vault account?
Click the upload icon to start uploading a document. In the file upload dialog box, locate the file from your local drive and select 'open' to 
complete the uploading.
To assign a document type to your uploaded file, click 'select doc type'.
This will show a pop up with a drop down selection of various document types. Choose the appropriate document type and click 'save'.
You can also edit the name of the file using the edit icon next to the filename.

What type of files can be uploaded?
File types that can be uploaded - pdf, jpeg & png.

What is the maximum allowed file size that can be uploaded?
Maximum allowed file size is 10MB.
